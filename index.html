Bill sharing the way it was meant to be.

Koha is a bill-sharing mobile app that simplifies your social life. Take turns footing the bill without ever having to play banker! With just a few taps, Koha tells you who should pay the bill next based on your previous transactions. Simplify your life & live generous with Koha.
